What Is a Pallet Jack?

A pallet jack is a common warehouse tool used for lifting and moving palletized goods.

It has two long forks that slide under a pallet and a hydraulic pump that helps lift the load a few inches off the ground.

Workers can then push or pull the pallet to a new location.

Pallet jacks are easy to operate, require no electricity, and are essential for everyday material handling in stores, factories, and distribution centers.
